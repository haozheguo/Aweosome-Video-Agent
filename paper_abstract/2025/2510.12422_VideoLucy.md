# VideoLucy: Deep Memory Backtracking for Long Video Understanding

- arXiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.12422
- Code: https://videolucy.github.io/

## Abstract (English, Original)
Recent studies have shown that agent-based systems leveraging large language models (LLMs) for key information retrieval and integration have emerged as a promising approach for long video understanding. However, these systems face two major challenges. First, they typically perform modeling and reasoning on individual frames, struggling to capture the temporal context of consecutive frames. Second, to reduce the cost of dense frame-level captioning, they adopt sparse frame sampling, which risks discarding crucial information. To overcome these limitations, we propose VideoLucy, a deep memory backtracking framework for long video understanding. Inspired by the human recollection process from coarse to fine, VideoLucy employs a hierarchical memory structure with progressive granularity. This structure explicitly defines the detail level and temporal scope of memory at different hierarchical depths. Through an agent-based iterative backtracking mechanism, VideoLucy systematically mines video-wide, question-relevant deep memories until sufficient information is gathered to provide a confident answer. This design enables effective temporal understanding of consecutive frames while preserving critical details. In addition, we introduce EgoMem, a new benchmark for long video understanding. EgoMem is designed to comprehensively evaluate a model's ability to understand complex events that unfold over time and capture fine-grained details in extremely long videos. Extensive experiments demonstrate the superiority of VideoLucy. Built on open-source models, VideoLucy significantly outperforms state-of-the-art methods on multiple long video understanding benchmarks, achieving performance even surpassing the latest proprietary models such as GPT-4o.

## 中文翻译
近期研究表明，利用大语言模型（LLM）进行关键信息检索与整合的智能体系统，已成为长视频理解的有前景方案。然而这类系统面临两大挑战：其一，通常在单帧级别建模与推理，难以捕捉连续帧的时间上下文；其二，为降低密集帧级描述成本，常采用稀疏采样，可能丢失关键细节。为解决这些问题，我们提出 VideoLucy——一种面向长视频理解的深度记忆回溯框架。受人类由粗到细回忆过程启发，VideoLucy 采用渐进粒度的分层记忆结构，显式定义不同层级记忆的细节程度与时间范围。通过基于智能体的迭代回溯机制，VideoLucy 系统性地挖掘全视频范围内与问题相关的深层记忆，直到收集到足够信息以给出高置信答案。该设计在保留关键细节的同时，实现了对连续帧时间关系的有效理解。此外，我们提出新的长视频理解基准 EgoMem，用于全面评估模型在超长视频中理解随时间展开的复杂事件及捕捉细粒度细节的能力。大量实验验证了 VideoLucy 的优越性。基于开源模型构建的 VideoLucy 在多个长视频基准上显著超越现有最优方法，性能甚至超过 GPT-4o 等最新闭源模型。
