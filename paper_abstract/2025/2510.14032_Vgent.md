# Vgent: Graph-based Retrieval-Reasoning-Augmented Generation For Long Video Understanding

- arXiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.14032
- Code: https://xiaoqian-shen.github.io/Vgent/

## Abstract (English, Original)
Understanding and reasoning over long videos pose significant challenges for large video language models (LVLMs) due to the difficulty in processing intensive video tokens beyond context window and retaining long-term sequential information. Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) has demonstrated effectiveness in processing long context for Large Language Models (LLMs); however, applying RAG to long video faces challenges such as disrupted temporal dependencies and inclusion of irrelevant information that can hinder accurate reasoning. To address these limitations, we propose Vgent, a novel graph-based retrieval-reasoning-augmented generation framework to enhance LVLMs for long video understanding. Our approach introduces two key innovations: (i) It represents videos by structured graphs with semantic relationships across video clips preserved to improve retrieval effectiveness. (ii) It introduces an intermediate reasoning step to mitigate the reasoning limitation of LVLMs, which leverages structured verification to reduce retrieval noise and facilitate the explicit aggregation of relevant information across clips, resulting in more accurate and context-aware responses. We comprehensively evaluate our framework with various open-source LVLMs on three long-video understanding benchmarks. Our approach yielded an overall performance improvement of 3.0%~5.4% over base models on MLVU, and outperformed state-of-the-art video RAG methods by 8.6%.

## 中文翻译
由于超出上下文窗口的大量视频 token 难以处理，且长程时序信息难以保留，大型视频语言模型（LVLM）在长视频理解与推理中面临显著挑战。检索增强生成（RAG）已在大语言模型长上下文处理中证明有效，但将 RAG 用于长视频会遇到时序依赖被破坏、无关信息混入等问题，影响准确推理。为此我们提出 Vgent：一种新的图结构检索-推理增强生成框架，用于提升 LVLM 的长视频理解能力。该方法有两点关键创新：（i）以结构化图表示视频，保留跨片段语义关系，从而提升检索效果；（ii）引入中间推理步骤，借助结构化验证降低检索噪声，并显式聚合跨片段相关信息，得到更准确、更具上下文感知的回答。我们在三个长视频理解基准上，结合多种开源 LVLM 进行了全面评测。结果显示，该方法在 MLVU 上较基础模型整体提升 3.0%~5.4%，并较当前最优视频 RAG 方法提升 8.6%。
